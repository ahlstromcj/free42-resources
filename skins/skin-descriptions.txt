Descriptions of Free42 Skins
Chris Ahlstrom
2016-02-01

Skins in Free42 Package:

   Standard.layout (built-in) Ugly X-Windows version, but small and usable.

   Realistic:                 Full HP42S rendition, a bit small for a
                              high-resolution screen.

   Andy480x800.layout:        A large-keyed skin in the style of the HP
                              financial calculators, a little bit too schematic,
                              but very readable.

   Ehrling42sl.layout:        Same as "Realistic".

   Ehrling42sm.layout:        A condensed version of "Realistic".

   HP-41.layout:              A garish style, vaguely like a TI calculator.

   kacskin.layout:            A condensed skin similar to "Realistic".
   kacskin_yellow.layout:     Looks the same as "kacskin" to me.

   KD0GLS_Compact.layout:     Similar to "kacskin" with an orange display.

   Khor.layout:               A small unretouched scan of an HP-42S

   KR.layout:                 Very compact DOS-font realistic rendition.

   Michaels-HP.layout:        Looks identical to "kacskin".

   Original.layout:           A black and white primitive skin.

   SemiAuto42b.layout:        Similar to "Khor", but blueish.
   SemiAuto42.layout:         Ditto.
   SemiReal42.layout:         Ditto.

   Voyager42.layout:          Extremely condensed version.

   Widgi42.layout:            Similar to "Standard", with small display.
